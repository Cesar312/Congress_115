Members of 115th Congress


Visualizations of Data

Using the python df.describe() function we deduced that the average age of congress members is a little over 60 years. The youngest member is 35 years of age and the oldest member is 90 years of age.
Further more using Matplotlib.pyplot histograms for visual representation we uncovered that most of the congress members were within 50-70 years of age.
